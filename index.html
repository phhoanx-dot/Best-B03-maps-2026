Why black ops 3 zombies?

The reason for Black Ops 3 Zombies is that, in my opinion, it has the best zombie game mode. I know that people are big fans of the black ops 2 maps because of the original Origins map and Mob of the Dead, which is a fan favorite to everyone, even me. But black ops 3 provides the original 6 maps and the additional Zombies Chronicles map, which provides all of the black ops maps starting from World at War to black ops 2.

A Feature that Black ops 3 provides that makes the game more fun

The different features that made the game more fun are the Gobblegum system, which involves rolling different Gobblegums to give you different abilities or maybe a perk that you would use in the game to help you go through the maps much longer than you could without using the Gobblegum. Some of the perks in the game change differently, such as double-tap one increases your gun's fire rate while double-tap 2 would have you shoot 2 bullets instead of 1, making for higher damage output and giving you the ability to do more damage while having the same amount of ammo provided in your gun. Another feature that they added into the game was modded maps that people in the community on Steam made, giving different modes and maps that are unique and fun to play with friends or by yourself, but modded maps are for pc player only
